About us

A local NGO focused on practical care and public awareness.

Garia Welfare Society works with communities to improve awareness around health, education and environmental responsibility. The organization supports local people through camps, student outreach, awareness sessions and welfare activities.

Our approach is simple: listen to community needs, bring people together, and create small actions that can grow into long-term change.
